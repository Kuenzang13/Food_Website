Ema Datshi

Bhutan's National Dish

About the Dish

Ingredients:

  • Green chilies: Adjust the quantity based on your spice tolerance.
  • Preferably local Bhutanese cheese, but you can substitute it with feta or cottage cheese.
  • Butter: For richness.
  • Tomatoes: Diced, for added flavor.
  • Onions: Sliced thin.
  • Garlic: Optional, but it enhances the flavor.

Instructions:

  • Prepare the vegetables: Slice the chilies lengthwise, dice the tomatoes, and slice the onions.
  • Cook the veggies: In a pot, saut√© onions and garlic in butter, then add chilies and tomatoes. Let them cook gently.
  • Add cheese: Stir in the cheese, allowing it to melt and mix with the vegetables.
  • Simmer: Let the stew cook on low heat until the flavors come together and the cheese forms a creamy consistency.
  • Serve: Pair it with steamed red or white rice for a hearty meal.
