About the Recipe

Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • Vegetable oil, for cooking
  • Butter and maple syrup, for serving
Instructions
  • Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • Mix Wet Ingredients: Whisk milk, egg, butter, and vanilla.
  • Combine: Gently stir wet into dry until just mixed.
  • Heat Pan: Preheat a non-stick pan and oil lightly.
  • Pour & Cook: Use ¬º cup per pancake; cook until bubbles form.
  • Flip: Cook other side until golden.
  • Serve: Top with butter, syrup, or berries.
